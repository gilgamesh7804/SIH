Culture & Traditions
Shillong is known as the "Rock Capital of India" due to its vibrant rock music scene and numerous local bands.


The city is often called the "Scotland of the East" because of its scenic beauty, lush green landscapes, and cool climate, reminiscent of Scotland.


Shillong is home to one of the oldest golf courses in India, established during British colonial rule.


The city hosts the Shillong Chamber Choir, which gained fame by winning "India's Got Talent" and regularly performs at various national and international events.


Elephant Falls, named for the shape of a rock resembling an elephant's head, is a popular spot and one of the most photographed waterfalls in the region.
